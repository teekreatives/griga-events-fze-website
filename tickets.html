HOW TO BUY A TICKET

Enter your details, choose a payment method, pay, then send proof via WhatsApp so we can verify and deliver your ticket.

Step-by-step

  1. Click Proceed to Checkout and enter your name, email, and phone number.
  2. Review your ticket order, then select Stripe or a manual payment method.
  3. For card: click Pay via Card in the Stripe panel, complete payment, then Send proof via WhatsApp.
  4. For M-Pesa, Botim, or bank transfer: pay using the listed details, copy if needed, then Send proof via WhatsApp with your screenshot.

We validate every payment, then email or WhatsApp your ticket with QR code once everything checks out.
