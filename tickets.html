HOW TO BUY A TICKET

Pick a payment method, send the money, then confirm it through the matching form so we can validate and hand over your ticket.

Step-by-step

  1. Stripe opens a secure checkout; complete the payment and keep the confirmation email or receipt.
  2. For BOTIM/M-Pesa, send the amount to the listed number and click “I have paid” to open the WhatsApp form.
  3. Attach a screenshot (proof of payment) and send. Your ticket will be sent immediately after confirmation.

We validate every payment, then email/WhatsApp you the ticket with QR code once everything checks out.
