About GRIGA Events FZE

Griga Events FZE is a UAE-based events company founded in 2025 by Grishon Gachomo. We’re the team behind experiences like Where There’s Smoke UAE and Murima Night, a cultural celebration loved by the Kikuyu community in the UAE.

What drives us is simple: bringing people together through great food, music, culture, and unforgettable moments. We’re passionate about creating events that feel alive, meaningful, and full of good energy — and we’re always working on new ideas to keep the experience fresh. At Griga Events FZE, every event is a memory in the making. Let’s create something amazing.
